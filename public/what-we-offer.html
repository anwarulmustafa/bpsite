Market Enforcement & Raids

Customs Surveillance & Seizures

We provide comprehensive customs monitoring and enforcement solutions to prevent the entry of counterfeit, look-alike, or unauthorized gray market products into Saudi Arabia. Our customs liaisons work closely with Saudi Customs to register trademarks, provide product identification training, and assist in real-time inspections.

Using data analytics and shipping intelligence, we identify suspicious import trends, track high-risk shipments, and alert customs checkpoints for interception. Once goods are seized, we offer legal assistance and coordination with authorities for prosecution or destruction.

Our proactive engagement with customs offices across major ports — including Jeddah, Riyadh, Dammam and Al Batha — enhances the early detection of IP-infringing imports, and helps protect the local supply chain from infiltration by counterfeit or unauthorized goods.

Gray Market Investigations

Gray market goods — while genuine — are sold through unauthorized channels, undermining official distributors and causing pricing inconsistencies and warranty complications. Our investigations trace the source, route, and local resellers of such products in Saudi Arabia.

We identify import loopholes, document parallel trade practices, and provide actionable intelligence to clients and legal teams. This includes identifying price dumping practices, unauthorized bulk sales, and cases of manipulated labeling.

Our insights help brand owners take corrective measures — whether by blocking distributor access, filing legal notices, or working with customs to tighten import restrictions. The goal is to protect official channels, pricing structure, and market exclusivity.

Legal Support & Advisory

In addition to enforcement, we offer full-spectrum legal support covering trademark protection, cease & desist notices, enforcement documentation, and litigation assistance. Our legal advisors are experts in Saudi IP law and provide practical, enforceable strategies to deal with infringers.
